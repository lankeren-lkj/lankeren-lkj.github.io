Gemini

Collaboration with bosa ceramiche, 2025

Gemini is a modular design system exploring duality through geometry, material and interaction. The project was first presented as a prototype in April 2025, and subsequently developed into a commercial collaboration with the Italian ceramics brand Bosa Ceramiche .

Hexagonal Tiles — Hexa & Hexo

At the core of the collection are two hexagonal tiles with opposing visual languages. Hexa is defined by flowing, organic curves, while Hexo adopts precise, linear patterns. Identical in form yet different in expression, they generate an open system of planar compositions.

Gemini tiles, 2024 © Kejun Li

Vases — Octa & Octo

The vases Octa and Octo translate the flat tile system into three-dimensional form. Built from six hexagonal faces, they retain the dual language of curve and line, transforming pattern into volume and surface into object.

Gemini Vases, 2025 © Kejun Li

Mirror — Union of Opposites

When Hexa and Hexo are combined, they form a mirror that unites curved and linear elements within a single reflective surface. The mirror functions as a point of synthesis, where duality becomes a unified form.

Gemini mirror, 2025 © Kejun Li

Game — Open Interaction

Gemini also includes an interactive game composed of two magnetic hexagonal tiles and a steel panel. Through assembling and reconfiguring patterns, participants engage directly with the system, completing the work through play and interaction.

Gemini game, 2025 © Kejun Li
