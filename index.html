2025

Project: The Last Gesture

A nipple-activated light sculpture flips the gaze and expands into a ritual-like performance.

Press: Featured as one of the 'Five Bright Design Stars to Know Now' in the October Design Issue of Financial Times HTSI.

Award: Emerging Designer of the Year – Shortlist (2025). Wallpaper* China

Collaboration: Gemini

This collection with Bosa ceramiche explores duality through modular geometry, transforming flat hexagonal tiles into sculptural forms where curves and lines coexist in balance.

Exhibition: Neo-Baroqueism, Salonesatellite, Milan design week

A modular tile system that reimagines architectural ornament through geometry and user interaction, exploring how historical decorative languages can be revitalized with modern fabrication and participatory engagement. The work received a personal visit and appreciation from Maria Porro, President of Salone del Mobile.

2024

Collaboration: Cadenza

A hexagon-based ceramic tile capable of generating three-dimensional surfaces and spatial rhythms, originally developed through computational exploration, this collection was later adopted for commercial production and presented internationally. Developed in collaboration with De Marchi Verona and selected by Bugatti Home.

2023

Exhibition: A Tile and Its Thousand Possibilities, Alcova, Milan design week

This project explores how a single fixed tile can generate endless visual outcomes through random, playful tessellation, transforming an ordinary object into an interactive and self-exploring design experience.
